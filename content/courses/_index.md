## Teaching

I teach courses in social psychology, work and organizational psychology, and research methods at both undergraduate and graduate (Master’s) levels.

My teaching focuses on developing students’ analytical skills, methodological rigor, and ability to critically engage with scientific research.

---

## Teaching experience

- ~290 hours of teaching (undergraduate and graduate levels)  
- Courses delivered in French and English  
- Teaching experience at Université Côte d’Azur and Université Paris Cité  

---

## Teaching interests

I am particularly interested in teaching:

- Social and organizational psychology  
- Research methods and statistics  
- Applied psychology in real-world contexts (e.g., work, mobility, decision-making)
